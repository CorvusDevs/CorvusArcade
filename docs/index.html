Early alpha, one tested game

So far Corvus Arcade has only been built and tested with Battle.net and Overwatch on Apple Silicon. Other games and launchers are not supported yet and may not run at all. Expect breakage between updates. This is for experimentation, not dependable play.

The app

Your library, native to macOS

Features

A launcher, not a toolkit

One-click Overwatch

A curated setup builds a clean bottle, installs Battle.net, and launches straight toward a match. No configuration.

Your engine, bundled

Wine 11 with DXMT and Apple's D3DMetal ship inside the app. No CrossOver subscription, no Homebrew, no Terminal.

Per-game graphics

Choose DXMT or D3DMetal, GPTK 3 or GPTK 4, window mode, and a frame-rate cap for each game independently.

Alt-tab friendly

A windowed mode runs the game inside a Mac window, so you can Cmd-Tab to other apps without leaving the match.

Frame cap & FPS overlay

Cap the frame rate to cut heat and fan noise, enable MetalFX upscaling, and show a live FPS readout when you want it.

First-run system check

Confirms Rosetta 2, the bundled engine, and free disk space before you start, and installs Rosetta for you if it is missing.
